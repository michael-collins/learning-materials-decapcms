In this exercise, you will apply Image textures to a Chess board and pieces



## Learning Objectives

1. Practice rendering images for submissions

## Instructions


**For this assignment, you will submit 3 total images.**
<br>  
Develop and refine a single chess set based on observation over time, peer and instructor feedback.
<br>  
**Submit three renders of _this individual_ set to both _Canvas_ _and the corresponding discussion thread for review_.** 
<br>  Make sure that your assignment is titled in the following format:
**Last name, First name,** the word **"Lesson"** followed by the **Lesson number,** and the **word "Assignment"** followed by the **Assignment number.**
<br>  
**Example:** Brill Ian Lesson x Assignment x

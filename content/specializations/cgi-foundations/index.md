This foundational specialization introduces students to the core concepts and workflows of computer-generated imagery. Students will learn industry-standard terminology, basic 3D modeling techniques, and the principles of materials, lighting, and rendering that apply across all CGI disciplines.

## Lessons

History, Modeling, Texturing/UV, Lighting, Camera, Rendering

## Projects

- Modeling a chair
- Modeling a room

## Test-Out Requirements

1. Polygonal models have good edge flow
2. Models have appropriate polygon density
3. Textures have minimal stretching
4. UVs are clearly laid out and well packed
5. PBR materials are properly configured
6. Good file and asset organization
7. Scene is well lit using scene lights or emissive materials
8. Renders are high quality
9. Take and get a B+ or better on all lecture quizzes
